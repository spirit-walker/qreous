Overview

1/5

The Victoria district is undergoing a transformation to create one of the most exciting new commercial and cultural hubs in London. 62 Buckingham Gate, developed by Land Securities, is the gateway to this revitalized destination. The LSM-designed public spaces include the main lobby; situated on a prominent corner, this space opens up to Victoria and embraces the burgeoning neighborhood at street level, heightening the connection between the building and its urban surroundings. Every aspect of the lobby space - the transparent boundary, high ceilings, light fittings that span from inside to out, art works by Stuart Haygarth and Grayson Perry - enhances this connectivity.
